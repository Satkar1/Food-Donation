About Us

We believe no one should go hungry. We've been dedicated to rescuing surplus food from local restaurants, grocery stores, and farms, redistributing it to those in need in our community.
Our mission is to combat food waste while ensuring everyone has access to nutritious meals.
We are passionate about raising awareness around hunger and promoting sustainable practices.
